Education

I am a graduate at the Technical University of Moldova (TUM), having a bachelor's in robotics and studying forward to getting a master's degree in computer science.

Programming

I like different sorts of spheres of programming: systems, embedded, AI & ML (reinforcement learning is really cool!), front-end and back-end. I also know a bunch of languages: Kotlin and Java, Python, C, Rust, JS.. and a bit of C++ and Assembly. I would love to learn Haskell.

If someone asked me: "What is your favourite programming language?" I would answer without hesitation: Rust. It incorporates pretty much everything I love about programming languages! Default immutability, the ability to go low-level when needed, clean syntax (sometimes a bit verbose), many more other things and a good community.
